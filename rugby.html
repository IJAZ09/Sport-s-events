Rugby worldcup


Rugby World Cup is the premier rugby international union cup held every in years. First rugby international union cup was held in in 1987. In this tournment the world different countries team compete with each other. This tournment like other tournment has the pool stage and knockout stages.
New Zealand and south Africa are the well known team in rugby those who won the multiple titles. These countries are well known by the rugby. Rugby is very fast growing game in many countries and very popular in countries like france, Uk and most of European countries. It is also very favourite sport among Asian and American subcontinet countries too.
Recent rugby world tournment was held in France in 2023 won by the South africa and runner up team was the New Zealand. South Africa won the 2023 rugby cup and became the world most successfull rugby team in rugby history.
