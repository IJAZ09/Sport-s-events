FIFA CUP


FIFA is the international football association founded in 1904. FIFA held more events and main football event is FIFA WORLDCUP which happened in every four years.In FIFA cup football team from all over the world participate. FIFA cup has set role of ranking and decision which country team will participate in cup according to the world goal ranking.
FIFA manage all the cup and set roles for teams those who participate in cup. FIFA also promote the football game asll over the world. Football has the most fan in world over 3.5 billions.
